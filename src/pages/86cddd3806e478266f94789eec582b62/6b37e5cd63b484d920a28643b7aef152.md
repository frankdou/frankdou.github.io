IOS14，才支持webp，故需要兼容处理

```xml
<picture>
  <source srcset="
    /uploads/img_small.webp 1x,
    /uploads/img_big.webp 2x" type="image/webp">
  <source srcset="
    /uploads/img_small.jpg 1x, 
    /uploads/img_big.jpg 2x" type="image/jpeg">
  <img src="/uploads/img_small.jpg">
</picture>
```
